FAQ
Common questions about billing.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — the Annual plan includes a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel before then at no cost.
How do I cancel?
Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone and cancel from there. Your premium access continues until the end of the billing period.
Can I switch from monthly to annual?
Yes. Cancel your monthly plan and subscribe to the annual plan. Apple will prorate any unused time.
My payment isn't going through — what should I do?
If you're in Bangladesh, your debit card may have international online payments disabled by default. Contact your bank (Dutch-Bangla, BRAC, City Bank, etc.) to enable it, or use an Apple App Store Gift Card as an alternative. You can also email us and we'll help.
I already subscribed — why can't I access premium features?
Open NourFit → Profile → Restore Purchases. This re-links your App Store purchase to your account instantly and for free.
Do prices vary by country?
Yes. Apple adjusts prices based on your local App Store region and currency. The prices shown here are in USD. Your local price may differ.
