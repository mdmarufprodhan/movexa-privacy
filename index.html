Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: June 30, 2026
Download on the App Store
🔒

Movexa is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We never sell your personal data.

1

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address to create and manage your Movexa account.

Health & Fitness Data

With your explicit permission, Movexa reads and writes to Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count and active calories burned
  • Workout sessions (type, duration, calories)
Important: Your health data is used solely to display your fitness progress inside the app. It is never shared with advertisers or third parties.

Location Data

We request your location only when Ramadan Mode is enabled, to automatically detect your city for accurate Suhoor and Iftar prayer times. Location data is used in real-time on your device and is not stored on our servers.

Camera

We access your camera only to scan food barcodes or photograph meals for AI nutrition analysis. No images are stored or transmitted beyond what is needed for analysis.

Workout & Nutrition Logs

Workout sessions, nutrition entries, meal logs, body measurements, and notes you create are stored securely in Firebase Firestore, linked only to your account.

Usage & Analytics Data

We collect anonymised usage data (screens visited, features used, crash reports) via Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience. This data does not personally identify you.

Advertising Data

Movexa shows optional rewarded ads to free users via Google AdMob. Ads are only displayed when a user chooses to watch one in exchange for a feature. AdMob may use your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent on iOS.

Purchase Information

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store or have access to your payment card details.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all your personal data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

5

Children's Privacy

Movexa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
